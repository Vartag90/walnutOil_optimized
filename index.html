why is it important to know?
natural product
Walnut oil is a unique natural product. It contains many trace elements and useful substances.
vitamins and micro(macro)elements
The oil contains polyunsaturated fatty acids (linolenic and linoleic), retinol and carotenoids (in the human body they are converted into vitamin A). The oil has a high content of B vitamins, as well as C and E. Micro and macro elements, such as iodine, iron, calcium, copper, zinc, magnesium, etc., are vital for every human body, their amount in the oil is quite high.
Clean and Healthy
Walnut oil is actively used for versatile purposes, whether it is assistance in the prevention and treatment of diseases, home cosmetics or cooking.
medicine
Walnut oil can be a good prophylactic for people prone to atherosclerosis, heart and liver diseases, metabolic disorders and thyroid function.
